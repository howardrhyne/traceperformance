The Putting System

Read · Speed · Aim · Align · Putt

Master putting with a systematic approach that breaks down every putt into learnable, repeatable components.

Step 1

Intro to Green Reading

Learn to read greens like a professional with systematic approaches to slope, grain, and speed assessment. Master the fundamentals that will transform your approach to every putt.

Build confidence in your reads through practical techniques for understanding green conditions and break patterns.

Foundation lesson for all putting
Step 2

Speed & Distance Control

Develop precise distance control and speed management for putts of all lengths. Learn the relationship between stroke tempo, backswing length, and consistent speed delivery.

Focus on lag putting techniques, proper pace for breaking putts, and eliminating three-putts from 30+ feet.

Master speed for consistent results
Step 3

Aiming & Alignment

Perfect your setup routine with precise aiming and body alignment fundamentals. Learn to aim the putter face accurately and align your body for consistent stroke delivery.

Includes setup drills, alignment verification techniques, and building a repeatable pre-putt routine that works under pressure.

Precision setup for every putt
Advanced Training

Lag Putting Mastery

Eliminate three-putts with advanced lag putting techniques for long-distance putts. Learn proper speed control, break reading, and distance judgment for putts over 30 feet.

Build confidence on large greens with systematic approaches to pace and line selection.

Advanced distance control
